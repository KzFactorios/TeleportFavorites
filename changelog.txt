    - Multiple GPS/favorite-related updates back-to-back are handled more efficiently so the bar does not redraw more than necessary
    - Tag editor delete confirmation: confirm/cancel respond reliably; delete overlay sets player.opened to the confirm frame (setting opened to nil was closing the tag editor); confirmation frame uses modal=true; right-click on confirm/cancel allowed; shift+click still allowed on those buttons

    - Retooled criteria for determining surface properties
    - ups optimizations
